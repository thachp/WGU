What is a vacation?

A vacation or holiday is a leave of absence from a regular occupation, or a specific trip or journey, usually for the purpose of recreation or tourism.

Why you should take vacation?

  1. You reduce your stress
  2. You do your health a lot of good
  3. You improve your productivity
  4. You become happier
  5. You are open to new perspectives
